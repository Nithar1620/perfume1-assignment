Our Features

A perfume is a very complex composition of top notes, heart notes and base notes.

Top notes

Top notes are the first scent so,it is also known as "head notes".Top notes are typically fresh, crisp, and energetic, and can be citrusy, floral, green, or herbal.

Heart notes

Heart notes are usually made up of a variety of ingredients, such as spices, florals, and fruits. They are often richer and more complex than top notes.

Base Notes

Base notes are rich, heavy, and smooth, and they are made up of large, heavy molecules that evaporate slowly.It appear after 30 minutes.
